# CAT (MBA) Preparation Resources

## About CAT

* **Exam**: Common Admission Test
* Conducted by: Indian Institutes of Management
* Purpose: Admission into MBA/PGDM programs in top business schools

---

# Recommended Books

## Quantitative Aptitude (QA)

### Quantitative Aptitude for CAT

* Author: Arun Sharma
* Publisher: McGraw Hill
* Key Features:

  * Covers fundamentals to advanced topics
  * Large number of practice questions

---

## Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension (VARC)

### Word Power Made Easy

* Author: Norman Lewis
* Key Features:

  * Strong vocabulary building
  * Improves reading comprehension

---

### How to Prepare for VARC for CAT

* Key Features:

  * Reading comprehension strategies
  * Verbal reasoning practice

---

## Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (DILR)

### Logical Reasoning and Data Interpretation for CAT

* Author: Nishit K. Sinha
* Key Features:

  * High-quality CAT-level practice sets

# Recommended Study Plan

```md
1. Begin with Quantitative Aptitude fundamentals (Arun Sharma)
2. Practice vocabulary daily (Word Power Made Easy)
3. Solve DILR sets consistently
4. Work on previous year questions weekly
5. Attempt 30–40 full-length mock tests before the exam
```
