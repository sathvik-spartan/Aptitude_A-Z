# CAT (MBA) Preparation Resources

## About CAT

* **Exam**: Common Admission Test
* Conducted by: Indian Institutes of Management
* Purpose: Admission into MBA/PGDM programs in top business schools

# Recommended Study Plan

```md
1. Begin with Quantitative Aptitude fundamentals (Arun Sharma)
2. Practice vocabulary daily (Word Power Made Easy)
3. Solve DILR sets consistently
4. Work on previous year questions weekly
5. Attempt 30–40 full-length mock tests before the exam
```
